Scintillating

Expounding knowledge through the blockchain.

At Scintillating, we work to try and solve the world's problems using the power of the blockchain. Since the dawn of cryptocurrencies, they are usable as a tool for safe, open transactions.

The computing power of the blockchain can be utilized to issue easier transactions from peer to peer, which is quite helpful in solving multiple problems. Thus, we are trying to bring the power of the blockchain to solve an everyday person's (like ourselves) problems.

Our Core Product



Introducing our flagship product: reBlock - A better study managment system based on the Ethereum Blockchain. Scientific advancment previously held back by bureaucracy and secrecy can be solved through open and distributed technology.

reBlock solves this by making the process to discover particpants, manage data, and secure this data far simpler, enabling researchers to truly focus on their work. Check out the website to learn more, and see our future plans.

About Scintillating

Scintillating: Clever, Amusing, Exciting

from Latin scintilationem

Scintillating is a company headed by a group of young, entrepreneurial leaders who are dedicated to working towards a beneficial change to current systems through the power of the Ethereum blockchain. Based in Massachusetts, our core belief comes from producing qualilty, quantity and speed, but value quality over all else.
