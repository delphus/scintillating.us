Scintillating

Expounding knowledge through the blockchain.

At Scintillating, we work to try and solve the world's problems using the power of the blockchain. Since the dawn of cryptocurrencies, they are usable as a tool for safe, open transactions.

The computing power of the blockchain can be utilized to issue easier transactions from peer to peer, which is quite helpful in solving multiple problems. Thus, we are trying to bring the power of the blockchain to solve an everyday person's (like ourselves) problems.

Thank you for your interest.

Warning: Scintillate.me and scintillating cryptotokens are in pre-production and have not officially been released yet. Expect broken or missing functionality.

About Scintillating

Scintillating: Clever, Amusing, Exciting

from Latin scintilationem

Scintillating is composed of a group of students dedicated to effecting change in past and future using the Ethereum blockchain, ultimately allowing for a brighter future. We are based in Massachusetts.
